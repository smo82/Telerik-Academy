Some of the packages needed for this Solution are deleted due to oversize.

You may restore missing NuGet Packages for the Solution by following the steps:

1. Open the solution
2. Right-click on Solution -> choose "Manage NuGet Packages for Solution"
3. Click "Restore"
4. Rebuild the solution when packages are restored

Possible errors
--------------------

- If the following error message appears

"An error occurred while trying to restore packages. Please try again" 
- you have to reinstal the missing packages by hand
